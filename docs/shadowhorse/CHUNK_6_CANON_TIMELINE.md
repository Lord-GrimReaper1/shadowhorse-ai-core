# Chunk 6 - Canon Timeline & Evolution of the Vision

## Purpose

This records how Shadowhorse doctrine emerged so future teams understand why decisions exist, not just what they are.

## Timeline

- T0: Foundational intent and Crossroads began as a co-op-forward game concept.
- T1: Studio identity formed around mythic, scientific, and human balance.
- T2: AI doctrine formed around human authority, truth, and safety.
- T3: Safety hardening added explicit red lines and non-negotiable limits.
- T4: Architecture converged into a human intent layer, reasoning layer, and execution layer.
- T5: Crossroads became the proving ground for social systems, trust, and worldstate simulation.
- T6: Embodiment was reframed as an interface, not an identity.
- T7: Canon consolidation began across Copilot and ChatGPT source chunks.
